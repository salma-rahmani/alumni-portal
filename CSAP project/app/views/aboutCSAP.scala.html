By this application we can collect all the Computer Science graduated students around the country and have access to them because this system stores all the information about the students.
also By this application we can put an announcement of our latest updates, scholarships, and job offers of Computer Science Faculty in it to notify all the students about new opportunities so by this the students can apply to their favorite section and we can help our people and solve their problems.
Keeping a clear line of communication between graduated students and the faculty of computer science is a very big challenge to be solved and it can be taken care of using several methods. Our proposed method is a solution that can be offered and keep the communication line clear between both of them closely.
Another performance of this platform form is that the Computer Science faculty can provide Alumni for Computer Science students and hold annual seminars for graduated students to speak about their experiences and problems that they encountered after graduation .
The target audience for this project is students of Computer Science who are graduating this year. Those who have already been graduated will be informed of the system once it has been implemented and its aspects have been fully tested.
